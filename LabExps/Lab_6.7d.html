(6.47) eF [n] = F  –1 {F { h[n] } • F { e[n] }}
where F {•} is the Fourier transform operator. Examine and listen to -- seconds of e[n], the original signal, and eF[n], the frequency-domain filtered result.
  1. How do you explain the change in the probability distribution of the amplitudes?

  2. Can you hear the difference between e[n] and eF[n]?
