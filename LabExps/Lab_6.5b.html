  2. When you listen to each of the signals, do you hear the result of the filtering? How does this filtered result compare to the filtered Gaussian noise of the previous section?

  3. How does the loudness of u[n] and uF [n] compare to the loudness of g[n] and gF [n] from the previous section? As before, they have been normalized after analysis but before being converted to audio WAV files.

  4. How do you explain the change in the estimates of the probability density function between u[n] and uF[n]?
