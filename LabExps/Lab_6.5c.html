Laboratory Exercise 6.5c (conclusion)

  2. When you listen to each of the signals e[n] and eF [n], do you hear the result of the filtering? How does this filtered result compare to the resuts from the previous sections?

  3. How does the loudness of e[n] and eF[n] compare to the loudness of the results from the previous sections?

  4. How do you explain the change in the estimates of the probability density function between e[n] and eF [n]?

  5. Can you estimate H(Ω=0)? Explain your estimate
